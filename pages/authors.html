Author Biographies

Learn about Frank Herbert, Brian Herbert, and Kevin J. Anderson - the masterminds behind the Dune universe.

Frank Herbert

1920-1986

Frank Herbert was an American science fiction author best known for the 1965 novel Dune and its five sequels. Herbert's work is known for its complex plots, political intrigue, and ecological themes. Dune won the Hugo Award and the inaugural Nebula Award for Best Novel.

Key Achievements:

  • Hugo Award Winner (1966)
  • Nebula Award Winner (1965)
  • Science Fiction Hall of Fame Inductee
  • Author of the original Dune Chronicles
"The spice must flow."

Brian Herbert

1947-Present

Brian Herbert is the son of Frank Herbert and has continued the Dune saga with Kevin J. Anderson. He has co-authored numerous Dune novels and maintains the legacy of his father's universe while expanding it with new stories and characters.

Key Works:

  • Prelude to Dune trilogy
  • Legends of Dune trilogy
  • Heroes of Dune series
  • Great Schools of Dune trilogy
"I'm honored to continue my father's legacy and expand the Dune universe."

Kevin J. Anderson

1962-Present

Kevin J. Anderson is a prolific science fiction author who has co-written many Dune novels with Brian Herbert. He is known for his ability to capture the essence of Frank Herbert's original vision while adding new depth to the universe.

Key Works:

  • Saga of Seven Suns series
  • Dune prequels and sequels
  • Star Wars expanded universe novels
  • X-Files novels
